GAME: Rock-Paper-Scissors

Instructions

This game is played with two players

The objective of the game is to beat your opponent

The two players will randomly make one of three hand signs;
- a rock (by making a fist)
- a paper (by making your hand flat
- or a scissors (by pointing out your index and middle fingers

If both players play the same thing, then they play again.
